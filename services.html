Our services

The full list of what we do.

Some projects need one thing. A lot of them need several. Here is the full list of the work we handle so you can see where your project fits.

Fiberglass Pool and Spa Installation

Pool and spa placement planned with the patio, grade, traffic flow, privacy, and the rest of the yard from the start.

Landscape Design and Installation

Planting plans, curb appeal upgrades, privacy screening, low-maintenance ideas, and full installation built around how you want the property to feel.

Irrigation Design and Installation

Irrigation systems planned and installed to support the landscape and help the property stay healthier over time.

Landscape Lighting

Lighting that improves evening visibility, adds atmosphere, and helps the architecture and planting read better after dark.

Retaining Walls

Structural and decorative retaining walls that solve grade changes and feel like part of the larger design.

Grade, Seed, Hydro-Seed and Sod

Rough grade, finish grade, seeding, hydro-seeding, and sod work that gets the site ready and the lawn established correctly.

Prairie Restoration and Prescribed Burns

Restoration work and maintenance for properties that need a more natural approach and long-term stewardship.

Artificial Turf

Artificial turf solutions for spaces where a traditional lawn is not the best fit or where lower maintenance is the goal.

Fire Pits

Gas or wood fire pits built into the yard so the gathering space feels finished and easy to use.

Brick Edge, Patio and Pathways

Patios, pathways, and edging that improve movement through the yard and give the landscape cleaner structure.

Lawn Drainage Issues

Standing water, poor runoff, and problem areas diagnosed and corrected so the yard works the way it should.

Outdoor Kitchen

Outdoor kitchen spaces planned with the patio, utilities, circulation, and overall layout in mind.

How people usually come to us

Most homeowners do not show up with a perfect category. They show up with a problem or a goal.

“We have water where we do not want it.”
Drainage and grading often set the direction for the rest of the project.
“The front of the house needs help.”
That can turn into design, planting, edging, lighting, irrigation, and lawn work.
“We want to finally use the backyard.”
That might mean a patio, retaining wall, fire pit, outdoor kitchen, pool, or several things working together.
Need help sorting it out?

You do not need to know the exact label before you reach out.

Tell us what the property is doing now, what you want it to do better, and what kind of finished space you are after. We can help sort out the right mix of services from there.
