About us

Local roots, practical experience, and a long view of how outdoor work should be done.

Lawns Unlimited of Iowa has been serving the Quad Cities since 1980. That history shows up in how projects are planned, how plant material is chosen, and how complicated yards are approached.

Who we are

We are a family-run company with a full-service nursery and a practical way of looking at outdoor work.

That means we care about how the project looks, but we also care about the parts that make it last: grade, drainage, layout, plant choices, and how the space will actually be used year after year.

We started in 1980 and have been based on Tanglewood Road since 1990, right by the Bettendorf YMCA. Those local roots matter because every property has its own quirks, and experience helps you read them faster.
