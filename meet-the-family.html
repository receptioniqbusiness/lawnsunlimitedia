Meet the family

The people behind the work.

Lawns Unlimited has always been a family business. The people planning the work are the same people who grew up in it, built it, and still care how the finished project turns out.

Family-owned since 1980

Different strengths, one company, and a long memory for how yards in this area really behave.

Some projects start with drainage. Some start with design. Some start with a backyard people want to finally use. What ties everything together here is that the planning and the field experience have stayed connected.

Marty

Built from field experience.

Marty started working in his family business at the age of 16, rough grading, finish grading, solving drainage issues, seeding and sodding until he married in 1980. Because of that early training, he is called on by the federal government, local cities, and schools to help resolve difficult drainage issues.

He has also worked alongside contractors and developers to help design residential subdivisions that meet the needs of the community. That practical drainage and grading background still shapes the way projects are approached today.

Cyndi

Design that starts with how people actually live.

Cyndi has worked alongside her husband to build Lawns Unlimited since May of 1980, with the company incorporating in July of 1987. She specializes in turf grass, plant species growth habits, physical needs, and urban adaptability, using that knowledge to shape a landscape design around your vision, lifestyle, and maintenance needs.

Clients receive a personalized color plan-view design along with a pictorial plant portfolio of the species recommended for their yard. Your input and questions during the design phase are encouraged, because the goal is a yard that feels like yours. Cyndi and Brett also meet with concrete contractors to help guide the flow of sidewalks and driveways, and on planting day she arrives with the crew to place trees, shrubs, and bed lines so the details land where they should.

Brett

The one handling the physical side of the project.

Brett performs the physical aspects of the work and specializes in rough grade, finish grade, irrigation design and installation, drainage, retaining walls, natural stone, brick patios, fire pits, outdoor kitchens, fiberglass swimming pools, and landscape lighting.

He grew up in the family business, spending his childhood at the tree nursery, running equipment, and helping with the daily work that kept the nursery going. That background turned into a real interest in what equipment can do and how grade can be shaped to create functional landscapes that still feel natural, whether that means fixing a drainage problem, contouring dirt, or building berms for privacy.
